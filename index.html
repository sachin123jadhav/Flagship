From Classroom to Startup: Nurturing the Next Generation of Innovators

Date-19th Nov 2023

Description

Welcome to the webinar "From Classroom to Startup: Nurturing the Next Generation of Innovators." Our 30-minute webinar aims to bridge the gap between the classroom and the startup world for students, offering practical insights, success stories, and guidance on how to turn ideas into real businesses.

Why attend this webinar:
Attend this webinar to explore a whole world of possibilities that lies beyond the classroom, to gain insights on overcoming entrepreneurial challenges, and to network with an experienced startup founder.

  • Get Expert Insights.
  • Explore $50k Funding Opportunity.
  • Highlights on AI tools in use.
  • Success Stories of teenpreneurs.
  • Networking Opportunities.
  • Interactive Q&A
  • Certificate of Participation.

Get inspired to start your venture.

About the Speaker:
Rachna Suneja | Founder- Flagship Club

Rachna Suneja is a dynamic and successful entrepreneur with extensive experience working across various high-level roles in global companies. Her exceptional business acumen and leadership skills set her apart in the startup world. Along with her inspiring journey, she brings a plethora of knowledge and expertise to the table.
